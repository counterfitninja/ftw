Who we are

About Frome Town Women FC
We are an independently run football club providing football for women, aged 16 and over, in and around Frome, Somerset. We are a 2* England Football Accredited club that compete in the South West Regional Women’s Football League, The Women’s FA Cup and the Somerset County Cup.

Frome Town Women are proud to be a true community club, offering free entry to our matches at Badger’s Hill where we promote a friendly but passionate atmosphere. Why not check out our fixture list, come and support the team, taking advantage of our tea hut and prize raffle while you are with us.
